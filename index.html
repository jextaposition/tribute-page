MR. SIDNEY POITIER

The first African-American (Bahamian) to win an Academy Award for Best Actor.


Time line of Mr. Sidney Poitier's Life:

  • 1927 - Born in Miami, Florida

  • 1964 - The first black actor to win the Academy Award for Best Actor

  • 1974 - Poitier was knighted by Queen Elizabeth II

  • 1997-2007 - Served as the Bahamian Ambassador to Spain

  • 2009 - Received the Presidential Medal of Freedom
